Frequently Asked Questions

Elimu Now is a Kenyan-based platform offering free online courses that cover subjects in the Kenyan school curriculum. Our mission is to provide quality education to learners across Kenya, empowering them with the knowledge and skills needed to excel academically. We cover subjects ranging from Mathematics, English, and Science to Kiswahili, Geography, History, and more.

Elimu Now offers free, high-quality courses specifically designed for Kenyan students, following the national curriculum. Our platform provides engaging lessons that are easy to understand and suitable for learners of all levels, from primary school to high school. We strive to make learning more accessible, affordable, and effective for all Kenyan students.

You can enroll in as many courses as you like on Elimu Now, at no cost! We encourage learners to explore various subjects to enhance their understanding and performance in school.

To enroll in courses, simply visit our website, create an account, and choose the course you want to take. You can register using your email or Google account. Once you're enrolled, you can start learning right away!

  • Kiswahili
  • Geography
  • History and Government
  • Business Studies
  • Computer Studies
